# ⚖️ Legal Mitra

> **AI-powered legal assistance for India.**

Legal Mitra is an open-source platform that uses AI to make legal help accessible, fast, and friendly for everyone. It offers:

- Multilingual legal Q&A
- Document generation
- Case tracking
- Lawyer and court tools

**Mission:** Solve India's justice crisis by empowering citizens, lawyers, and courts with technology.
